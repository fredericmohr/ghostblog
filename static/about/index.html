About

My name is Frederic Mohr, I work as an information security officer and I write about everything that seems noteworthy to me. So you might find workarounds or tutorials on my blog, but also project documentation, gadget reviews or anything else that comes to my mind. Even though I want to focus on information security, it might happen that you stumble across a Minecraft video or something similar that has somehow found its way onto my blog.

I think this is the best place to thank everyone, whose blog has enlightened me, or simply helped me finding the solution to an annoying problem . Also to all the users in countless boards who where not afraid to ask or to busy to answer all these question, saving me tons of time and frustration – especially to the people at all the great stackexchange.com sites (stackoverflow, security, etc.).
And last but not least to you, for reading my blog – or at least this site.

Thanks to all of you,

Frederic

A few words on the legal stuff

There are three links in this sites footer. The first one on the left links to my Impressum page, which contains some german legal mumbo jumbo that I have to put there. The second one on the left, links to my Datenschutzerklärung (which is privacy policy in german) and the third on the right, links to the english privacy policy.

The german Datenschutzerklärung and the english privacy policy are two totally different pages on the same matter (privacy), which I both don't like. Both pages are created using one of those many generators and therefore are limited in fitting this site. That's why I want to say a few words (no legal statements, but mere comments) on how I want to handle privacy here.

Social Plugins

As you might have noticed, by default there are no social sharing buttons on this site. You can enable them (javascript) by clicking on the blue button in the sidebar (not visible on mobile devices!). However, there is no option (yet) to remember this, so you have to enable manually each time you want to share a page.

One exception is the "recent tweets" panel, which automatically loads all the twitter social stuff.

Comments

I am using disqus as comment platform, but same as the social sharing buttons you have to enable disqus on every page manually.

Tracking

At the moment (29. Jan '14) there is no tracking implemented in this page. The only thing that uses cookies at all is (again) the twitter feed panel. I'm not sure if I will keep that though - time will tell.

Cookies

What cookies? There are non - yet. Again, except the ones from Twitter.

AdSense

There are no ads on this blog, so you can give your adblocker a break. I don't know if I ever enable ads, probably not. For me this blog is more about writing and free knowledge than about earning some cash.

But what about...?

I think I listed everything important. Let's just say that the links at the bottom are there, because they should be there not because I want them.

It's important to me that this site is secure and its content accurate. So if you ever come across something suspicious or have a question or concern than please contact me at admin@hashtagsecurity.com

Or if you just want to talk about security stuff - that's fine too.
